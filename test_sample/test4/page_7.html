Device Compatibility

iOS 26 requires A13 Bionic chip or newer, with Apple Intelligence features limited to devices with A17 Pro chip or newer.

iPhone 12 & 12 mini A14 Bionic

iPhone 11, 11 Pro, & 11 Pro Max A13 Bionic

iPhone XS, XS Max, and XR (A12 Bionic) are not supported
