# Apple Q1 2025 Earnings Analysis Report

## Executive Summary
Apple reported a record-breaking fiscal first quarter for 2025 (ending Dec 28, 2024).
- **Revenue**: $124.3 Billion (+4% YoY, All-time Record)
- **EPS**: $2.40 (+10% YoY, All-time Record)
- **Net Income**: $36.3 Billion (Record)
- **Stock Reaction**: Up ~3% in aftermarket trading, reaching ~$245.

## Key Performance Indicators
### Growth Drivers
- **Services**: $26.3B (+14% YoY). New record high.
- **Mac**: $8.99B (+15% YoY). Strong double-digit growth.
- **iPad**: $8.09B (+15% YoY). Strong recovery and growth.

### Challenges
- **iPhone**: $69.14B (-1% YoY). Slight decline, missed some expectations. First full quarter with iPhone 16.
- **China Market**: Revenue declined by 11%, due to local competition and AI feature regulatory delays.

## Future Outlook
- **AI Strategy**: Apple Intelligence features rolling out (more languages in April) are expected to drive future device upgrades.
- **Installed Base**: Crossed 2.35 billion active devices.
- **Guidance**: CEO Tim Cook called it the "best quarter ever".

## Stock Analysis
- Market reacted positively despite the iPhone miss, focusing on the robust Services growth and overall record profitability.
- The 3% rise suggests investor confidence in the long-term AI strategy.
